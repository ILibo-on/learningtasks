Godzilla

About Movie:

A movie about Godzilla for the celebration of the big Gs 50th anniversary in 2004

About the Plot:

The Earth Defense Force are locked in battle with a number of monsters attacking cities around the world, when a mysterious race of aliens known as the Xiliens arrive on Earth. Reviving a sealed Godzilla in ice may be the EDF's only chance to save the planet.
