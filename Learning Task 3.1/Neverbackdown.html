Never Backdown

Summaries:

A frustrated and conflicted teenager arrives at a new high school to discover an underground fight club and meet a classmate who begins to coerce him into fighting.

Jake Tyler was lured into an ultimate underground fighting club in a Backyard Fight at his new high school, where he finds a mentor in a mixed martial arts veteran. After receiving threats to his friends and family's safety, Jake seeks out the guidance of a seasoned fighter to prepare his mind and body for one final no-holds-barred elimination bout with his persistent personal enemy and local martial arts champion Ryan McCarthy.
