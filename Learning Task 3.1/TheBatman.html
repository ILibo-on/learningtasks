The Batman

About Movie:

Rurouni Kenshin (The Wanderer Kenshin or Kenshin the Wanderer) also known Samurai X is a Live Action Adaptation of the Anime with the same name, it's has four movies was originally written and drawn by Nobuhiru Watsuki.

About the Plot:

The story tells about the Batman that ventures into Gotham City's underworld when a sadistic killer leaves behind a trail of cryptic clues. As the evidence begins to lead closer to home and the scale of the perpetrator's plans become clear, he must forge new relationships, unmask the culprit and bring justice to the abuse of power and corruption that has long plagued the metropolis.
