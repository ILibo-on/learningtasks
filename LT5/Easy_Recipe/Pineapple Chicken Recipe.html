Pineapple Chicken Recipe

An Easy College Recipe for College Students to create an Authentic Pineapple Chicken!

In other terms it can also be called Pinanyahan Manok in Filipino-Tagalog

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp canola or sesame oil
  • 1 (10-oz) package frozen broccoli (or stir-fry vegetable mix), thawed*
  • 1/2 C stir-fry sauce
  • 1/4 C pineapple juice, use reserved juice from canned pineapple
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (optional - use if you like it hot!)
  • 1 (15-oz) can pineapple chuncks or tidbits, drained (reserve the juice!)
  • 2 C diced cooked chicken or 2(10-oz) cans chicken breast, drained and flaked

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over mediom high heat. Add all ingredients EXCEPT the pineapple & chicken. Cook and stir until heated through, 5-6 min.
  2. Add pineapple and chicke; cook another 2 minutes.

    3. Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta.

    *Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold running water for several minutes.
