Basic Pasta

An Easy College Recipe for College Students to create an Authentic Basic Pasta!

Some ingredients to make Basic Pasta

Ingredients

  • Dried Pasta... any work well
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese/Tuna/Both/None :)

Method

  1. Boil your Pasta adding salt to the water for later one.
  2. While pasta is cooking grate your cheese/drain your tuna.
  3. When the past is cooked add about half a tube of tomato puree, stirring well, to give you a passata consistency. Of course you can just use passata if you want, and drain the pasta instead.
  4. Then, add the tune if using any, stirring in well. Leave for a minute to heat the tune. Add salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Serve into a bowl and sprinkle lots of cheese on top :)

Enjoy!

This can be enjoyed as a simple tomato pasta, or with cheese or tune, or both :)
