Goat cheese/beetroot salad

A classic College Recipe - Simplified

Ingredients

  • Lettuce/ Any leafy salad base you like
  • Beetrot (cut into slices)
  • Walnuts (crush up abit)
  • 1Balsamic Dressing (you can also make your own if you know how lol)

Method

  • Put lettuce/ leafy salad on plate
  • Add beetroot and goats cheese on the top
  • Stirr in remaining ingredients.
  • Sprinkle crushed walnuts on top
  • Drizzle balsamic dressing over
